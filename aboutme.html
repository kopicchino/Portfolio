About Jefferson Padua

I am currently a second-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at the Technological University of the Philippines - Manila, where I have maintained a consistent position as a Dean's Lister. Alongside my academic pursuits, I am passionate about web design and enjoy honing my skills in this creative field.

Outside of academics, I hold the position of President of a Dance Company and serve as the Head of Technical at ARC Theater and Dance Company in Malabon City. As a performing artist, I actively compete and perform at local, national, and international levels, specializing in Contemporary and Jazz genres.

In terms of my academic achievements, I was honored to be the Class Valedictorian of my Senior High School batch (Class of 2024) at Arellano University Jose Rizal Campus. I have a deep passion for research, particularly investigatory research, and was proud to be recognized as the Overall Best in Research at the Research Colloquium 2024, across all Arellano University campuses.

Currently, I am expanding my knowledge by studying programming languages such as C, C++, Python, and Java. My diverse interests in technology, performing arts, and research fuel my drive to pursue excellence in all areas of my life.
